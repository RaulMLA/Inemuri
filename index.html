Inemuri

Select your destination

Inamuri is an application for people who like to sleep during public transportation trips.

The application allows you to set a destination and receive different notifications in the form of vibrations on your mobile device when you are approaching it.

To do this, click on Start and select the destination on the map. You can change the marker by dragging and dropping or simply by clicking on the new destination location.

Once selected, click on Save to set the destination and start receiving alerts.

If you have saved the destination and want to modify it, you can do so by clicking on Modify. To cancel the destination, click on Cancel.
